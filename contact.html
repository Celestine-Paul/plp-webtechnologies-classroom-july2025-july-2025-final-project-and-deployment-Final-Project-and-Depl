Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity varies by product category. Generally, it's 10 pieces for most items, but we can discuss flexible arrangements for new customers.

Do you offer custom sizing and designs?

Yes, we offer custom sizing and can work with you on custom designs for bulk orders. Additional charges may apply based on customization requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, cheques, Mpesa, and for established customers, we offer credit terms. Cash payments are accepted for local pickups.

How can I get a product catalog?

You can request our digital catalog by filling out the contact form above or calling us directly. We also have physical catalogs available upon request.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we primarily serve the Kenyan Market, but we are exploring international shipping options. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for defective items. Custom orders are non-returnable unless there's a manufacturing defect.
