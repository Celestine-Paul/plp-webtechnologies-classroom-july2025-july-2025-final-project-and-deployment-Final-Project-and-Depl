About Kaarupet Wholesaler

Your trusted partner in premium fashion at wholesale prices

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Kaarupet, Kapenguria, Kaarupet Wholesaler has been a leading supplier of premium fashion clothing for over a decade. We started with a simple vision: to make high-quality, fashionable clothing accessible to retailers and businesses at wholesale prices.

What began as a small family business has grown into a trusted wholesale partner for hundreds of retailers across Kenya and beyond. Our commitment to quality, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service has made us a preferred choice for fashion retailers looking for reliable suppliers.

We specialize in a wide range of clothing categories including casual wear, formal attire, women's fashion, and men's clothing. Every piece in our collection is carefully selected to ensure it meets our high standards of quality and style.

Our Mission

To provide premium quality fashion clothing at wholesale prices while maintaining exceptional customer service and building lasting partnerships with retailers worldwide.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and preferred wholesale fashion supplier globally, known for quality, reliability, and innovation in the fashion industry.

Our Values

Quality, integrity, customer satisfaction, and continuous innovation drive everything we do. We believe in building relationships that last.
